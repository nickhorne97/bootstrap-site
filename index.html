    • Climate Change

    Climate change, though it can occur naturally, is a process that has been sped up due specific human activity. There are many negative outcomes of climate change but there are steps we can take to reduce the impact immensely.

    Some people seem to celebrate the idea of some places becoming warmer, though this would cause many issues including extreme weather. Many species wont be able to adapt quick enough to survive the change. It only takes subtle changes as humans to slow down the process.

    What we do

    Evergreen is a non-profit organization that participates in many different things regarding the subject of climate change. We have speakers who visit many schools to try and raise awareness to young adults to try and make their futures a safer and nicer place. Young adults are large consumers of products that affect climate change and also tend to care less about their carbon footprint.

    We do raise money for research to try and find better and more scientific ways to not only measure the impact climate change will have but also to discover new ways to slow it down or even prevent it.
